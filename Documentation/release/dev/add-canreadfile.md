## CanReadFile support

CanReadFile support has been added or extented to the following readers:
 - vtkDataSetReader (added)
 - vtkXMLReader (extended to stream)
 - vtkXMLGenericDataObjectReader (added)
 - vtkPTSReader (added)
 - vtkCityGMLReader (added)
 - vtkSTLReader (added)
 - vtkHDFReader (extended to stream)
 - vtkOpenVDBReader (extended to stream)
 - vtkPLYReader (extended to stream)
 - vtkDIMCOMImageReader (extended to stream)
 - vtkHDRReader (extended to stream)
 - vtkJPEGReader (extended to stream)
 - vtkPNGReader (extended to stream)
 - vtkTGAReader (extended to stream)
 - vtkBMPReader (extended to stream)
