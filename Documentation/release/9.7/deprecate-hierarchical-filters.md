## Deprecated vtkHierarchicalBoxDataSet filters

The following filters that supported `vtkHierarchicalBoxDataSet` have been deprecated and will be removed in a future
release:

1. `vtkHierarchicalDataExtractLevel`
2. `vtkHierarchicalPolyDataMapper`
3. `vtkHierarchicalDataExtractDataSets`
4. `vtkHierarchicalDataLevelFilter`
